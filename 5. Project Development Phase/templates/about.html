About EduGenie

Intelligent study companion powered by state-of-the-art AI systems

Our Mission

At EduGenie, we believe that education should be personalized, interactive, and accessible. Our goal is to empower students and lifelong learners around the globe by providing an intelligent companion that can adapt complex concepts to their level of understanding, test their progress dynamically, and outline personalized paths to mastery.

Cloud Cognitive Layer

Integrates with Google Gemini API models to run advanced prompt engineering pipelines, generating structured JSON outputs for quizzes, roadmaps, explanations, and replies.

Local Execution

Leverages local Hugging Face Transformers. Utilizes LaMini-Flan-T5 models running natively in PyTorch on the host server to support offline text summarization.
